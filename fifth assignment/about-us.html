About us



Dawa Education Consultancy, represents overseas institutions in Bhutan, we have a long-standing reputation of being the most comprehensive study-abroad consultants and the first in the Kingdom.

We start the process with exhaustive career counseling. Our counselors advise you with career options and match individual student profiles as per academic background, aptitude and aspirations to suitable careers. We not only are knowledgeable about available options but offer advice based on first-hand experience by visiting foreign academic institutions.

Former student experiences are taken into account to make the process even more rigorous and productive.
Global Reach was awarded “Education Agency Of The Year” by PIE News.
